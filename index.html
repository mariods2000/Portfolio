Tiki Totems

Unity Pixel Art

Tiki Totems is a game I made in 3 hours. The tiki totems are constantly trying to fall over, and the goal is to click them to prevent them from falling!

Unreal Engine 3D

Unfallen was a side project where the goal was to learn how to use Unreal Engine. It was supposed to be a 3D RPG, but as you can see I got a bit carried away with archery in the game!

Kiwi Jump

Unity Pixel Art

Kiwi Jump is my personal take on Flappy Bird, it's pretty straightforward: tap the screen to avoid the obstacles. In this case, the Kiwi doesn't want to get blended, so tap away!

Elevator Despair

Unity Pixel Art

Elevator Despair was a game I made in 2 hours, an addictive highscore game where the higher up the building you go, the bigger the risk of falling down.

Whisk It

Unity Pixel Art

Whisk It was made during a Game Jam with a friend over 7 days. Despite being busy with University and work, we managed to create it successfully.
